Commission Policy

If you commission me, I promise to finish your commission within THREE DAYS of your request. If somehow this was not done or if the commission is not up to your standards, you get another commission free of charge.

As for refunds, for the first twenty-four hours after your commission, if you decide it was a mistake you get your money back in full. For every subsequent twenty-four hours, the amount you get back can be calculated with the following equation: m = c(1/2)^x, that is, every day you wait the money you receive gets halved. For example, if you canceled the commission on the second day you would get half the price back, and if you cancel on the third you only get a quarter refund. Once the picture is posted, there is no refund. Sorry if this is harsh, but I work hard on these and don't want the work to go to waste.
